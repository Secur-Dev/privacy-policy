Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 27, 2025

This privacy policy explains how Amazon Prime Price Sorter ("we", "us", "our") handles information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, search queries, or other user data. All operations are performed locally within your browser.

Permissions

The extension uses minimal permissions:

  • Host Permissions: Access to Amazon.com search pages only, to modify URL parameters for a better shopping experience.
  • DeclarativeNetRequest API: Used to update Amazon search results automatically, filtering by Prime and sorting by price.

Third-Party Services

We do not use or integrate any third-party services, analytics tools, or tracking technologies.

Changes to This Policy

If our practices change, we will update this Privacy Policy and notify users through the extension page or release notes where appropriate.
